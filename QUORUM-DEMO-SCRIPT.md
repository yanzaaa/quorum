# Quorum — demo script (record on the live site, ~2.5 min)

**Live URL:** https://quorum-ajoveyxaj-anthonyyanza-2773s-projects.vercel.app
Screen-record the page. Speak the **bold** lines; the cues in (parens) are what to click/point at.

---

**[0:00 — Hero, let the animation play]**
> **"This is Quorum. Most multi-agent demos celebrate when the agents agree. But three agents agreeing isn't authorization — a fluent council can still be confidently, catastrophically wrong about something irreversible. Quorum is a council that knows when a vote isn't enough."**
(Let the hero breathe — three voices converging on the verdict core.)

**[0:20 — click "Run the council on the queue"]**
> **"For every action, three Qwen agents deliberate: a Proposer argues for it, a Skeptic hunts for what could go wrong, and a Referee decides last — after hearing both."**

**[0:35 — point at "Refund a $40 duplicate charge"]**
> **"A clearly safe, reversible action — all three approve — so it just executes."**

**[0:48 — point at the $50k wire and 'delete the database' cards]**
> **"A $50,000 wire from an emailed invoice, and 'delete the customer database' — the council unanimously rejects both on its own. No human needed."**

**[1:05 — the $30 refund / 11 prior refunds card]**
> **"Now watch this one. A $30 refund — low-stakes and reversible, the kind a simple rule waves straight through. The Proposer approves… then hears the Skeptic flag eleven refunds this month, and changes its vote."** (point at *"↺ changed from approve after the skeptic"*)
> **"That's the society negotiating, not just tallying."**

**[1:28 — the money moment: the $12k fully-approved payment]**
> **"And here's the one that matters. A fully-approved $12,000 payment — all three agents vote to execute, at high confidence. And the guardrail holds it back anyway, and escalates."** (point at the held-back banner)
> **"A unanimous, fully-authorized council still doesn't get to pull an irreversible trigger without a human. Consensus is necessary — but never sufficient."**

**[1:52 — point at the headline metric near the top of the results]**
> **"Up here's the measurable part: a lone agent would have executed [read the number on screen] of these on its own — and the council stopped every one. That's the gain over a single agent."**
(The exact number varies per run — just read whatever it shows; qwen-max is cautious so it's often 1–2.)

**[2:08 — close]**
> **"It's a real three-agent loop on Qwen Cloud, with a deterministic guardrail that can only ever make the outcome safer. A society of agents that's safe because no vote — not even a unanimous one — can authorize the irreversible. That's Quorum."**

**[optional, if you have time — scroll to "Propose your own action"]**
> **"And it's not canned — type any action and send it to the live council."** (the form defaults to a $12k irreversible payment → it'll be held back too.)

---

### Tips
- Record at 1440-wide so the two-column cards and the hero both read.
- The strongest 20 seconds is the **$12k held-back** beat — slow down there.
- If a card is still "loading" when you reach it, pause; each card runs 3–5 live Qwen calls.
- Total spoken ≈ 2:15; with pauses it lands just under 3:00.
